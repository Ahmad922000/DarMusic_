About Us

Under the auspices of His Excellency Bishop John Ginbert, the shepherd of the diocese, the Dar Al Nahda Music Institute was established in November of 2020 under the supervision of Mr. Shadi Najjar, Director of the Institute. The idea of ​​establishing began based on the desire of His Eminence Bishop John Ginbert to work on creating a generation that cares about its talent and seeks for music to be a tool for expressing its expression. His personality and self, and through academic study in theoretical and practical music and on the musical instrument chosen by the student, the institute welcomes and includes all ages, starting from the age of 9 years until the ages of adults. Teaching includes Western and Eastern music. The Institute acquired a number of Western and Eastern musical instruments, and worked on cooperation and preparation with an important cadre of professional professors in the field of music and education. Over the course of several weeks, the Institute’s headquarters was prepared, located in the Al-Aziziya neighborhood near Al-Aziziya Square. To prepare the teaching staff for the curricula, the institute adopts the method of successive courses, and each course is 3 months, in which the student receives 12 theoretical lessons with the rest of his colleagues of the same age and musical level, and 12 practical lessons, each lesson alone with the supervising teacher, the duration of each lesson is a full hour, After completing the course, the student undergoes a theoretical and practical examination, and upon passing it, he can continue in the next course.
